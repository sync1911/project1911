Conversion Rate - store conversion rate = Total Sales / Total Outbound Clicks * 100

VC to ATC - rate from product view to adding to cart = Total Content Views / Total Add to Cart * 100

ATC to ITC - rate from adding the product to the cart to completing the order = Total Add to Cart / Total Initiate Checkouts * 100

ITC to PUR - rate from completing the order to purchasing = Total Initiate Checkouts / Total Orders * 100

Quality Click Percentage - rate of people who landed on our website and the pixel was triggered = Total Page View Website Events / Total Ad Clicks * 100

CTR = Percentage out of 1000 people who saw the ad, clicked

Purchases = Number of orders

ROAS = Total Sales / Total Spent

CPM = Cost of the ad to reach 1000 people
